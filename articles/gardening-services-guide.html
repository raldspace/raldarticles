Finding Professional Gardening & Landscaping Services in Kenya

Transform your outdoor space with professional landscaping. Learn about services offered, pricing, seasonal maintenance, and how to find reliable gardeners.

3. Seasonal Maintenance Calendar

Kenya's climate allows for year-round gardening, but each season has specific tasks:

JAN

Dry Season (Jan-Mar)

Focus on irrigation, mulching, and drought-resistant plant care. Prune trees before rains begin.

APR

Long Rains (Apr-Jun)

Prime planting season. Focus on weeding, pest control, and drainage management.

JUL

Cool Season (Jul-Sep)

Lawn recovery period. Aerate, fertilize, and overseed. Plan landscape projects.

OCT

Short Rains (Oct-Dec)

Second planting window. Maintain regular mowing and prepare for dry season.

4. What to Look for in a Gardener

A professional gardener should have:

  • Knowledge of Local Plants: Understanding of Kenyan flora and what grows well in your area
  • Pest Identification Skills: Ability to spot and treat common garden pests and diseases
  • Tool Maintenance: Well-maintained, professional-grade equipment
  • Reliability: Consistent schedule and communication
  • Insurance: Coverage for accidents or property damage

Interview Questions

  • • How long have you been gardening professionally?
  • • Can you provide references from current clients?
  • • What's your approach to organic/sustainable gardening?
  • • How do you handle plant diseases and pests?
  • • What happens if you can't make a scheduled visit?
