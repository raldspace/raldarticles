Frequently Asked Questions

How much is wedding photography in Kenya?

Professional wedding photography ranges from KES 40,000 to 120,000 depending on coverage hours, second shooter, and deliverables (album, prints, digital files).

Where can I find photographers near me?

Use Hudumika’s directory or Fimbo’s events & photography category. Filter by location (Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret).

Do photographers offer packages?

Yes. Most offer full day, half day, and custom packages. Many also bundle photo + video at a discount.

How long does photo delivery take?

Sneak peeks within 48–72 hours. Full gallery delivery typically 2–6 weeks depending on editing volume.

Do photographers travel outside Nairobi?

Most travel nationwide (Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Diani, Naivasha). Travel and accommodation fees may apply.

Is drone photography available?

Absolutely. Many pros offer drone photography Kenya for weddings, real estate, and large events. Confirm licensing and weather conditions.

Can I book same-day photography?

Last-minute bookings depend on availability. Always contact the photographer directly via WhatsApp or call to check.
