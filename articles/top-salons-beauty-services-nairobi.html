FAQs – Beauty Services in Kenya

How much is braiding in Kenya?

Knotless braids range from KES 2,500 to 5,000 depending on length, size, and complexity.

Where can I find makeup artists near me?

Use Hudumika’s directory or check Fimbo’s beauty listings. Filter by location (Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu).

Do beauty professionals offer home service?

Yes! Many mobile beauty pros offer makeup, nails, massage, and braiding at your home or hotel.

How long do acrylic nails last?

With proper care, 2–3 weeks before fill-in is needed.

How much is body massage in Kenya?

Professional massage (Swedish/deep tissue) costs KES 2,000–5,000 per hour at a salon or mobile.

Can I book beauty services same day?

Many pros accept same-day bookings, especially for nails, waxing, and makeup — contact directly via WhatsApp.

Are mobile beauty services available outside Nairobi?

Yes, in Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret and major towns. Always confirm travel fee if outside CBD.
